Rex: Explore the App World

I co-founded Rex with Danny Guo, Evan Leichter, and Alice Zheng through the TigerLabs Undergraduate Accelerator in 2012. Right before the academic year of 2012-13, Alice and I decided to commit to the project, raise funds, and see if we could turn it into a business.

Our goal was to build the easiest way to discover new Android apps. Rex provides personalized app recommendations and a unique, visual interface for exploring related apps.



PanesLibrary

PanesLibrary is an open-source library that makes it ridiculously easy to make native Android apps with flexible multi-pane tablet layouts. On the phone, the app appears as a conventional app with a sliding menu and a content pane where fragments are stacked on top of each other. On the tablet, the menu and all other fragments appear in dynamically added panes of varying sizes.

It's been starred >200 times, forked >50 times, and used in production!



AutoWallpaper
